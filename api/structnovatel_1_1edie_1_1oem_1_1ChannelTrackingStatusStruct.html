struct ChannelTrackingStatusStruct

Channel Tracking Status word fields decoded. Fields are from https://docs.novatel.com/OEM7/Content/Logs/RANGE.htm#TrackingState. Not every RANGECMP* message contains a raw form of the channel tracking status word. It is the case that it must be constructed from known data during the decompression process. However, it is also highly probably that there will be fields in the channel tracking status word that cannot be inferred based on the data in the RANGECMP* log, and certain defaults must be applied.
